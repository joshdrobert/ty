About This Model

This CNN model classifies skin lesions from dermoscopy images as either malignant or benign. The model was trained on the Skin Cancer Malignant vs Benign dataset from Kaggle, which contains dermoscopy images of various skin lesions.

Dataset: Skin Cancer Malignant vs Benign
